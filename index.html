What is Fake News?

Fake news is anoher form of news which purposely gives out false information to the public which could cause them to be mislead and have the wrong view on the information.

There are many reasons to why fake news is published on the internet:

1. False information which is delibertely published in order to achieve a reaction from the public and perhaps force them to change their views and opinions.

2. Information which is is somewhat true along the lines however, inlcude deliberate changes which are not true and this could cause tensions within the public to rise.

3. False information which is purposely set out in order to encourage the public to react a certain why which could be beneficial.
